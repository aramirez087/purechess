## pure

The piece SVGs in `pure/` are a **derivative of the cburnett set** above,
generated by `apps/web/scripts/build-pure-pieces.mjs`: the geometry is
unchanged; flat fills are replaced with ivory/graphite gradients and the
outlines/detail lines are recoloured to warm ink/bone tones.

- License: **CC-BY-SA 3.0** (same as the original, as required)
- Original author: Colin M.L. Burnett
- Modifications: PureChess (gradient fills, recoloured strokes)

## sculpted

The piece SVGs in `sculpted/` are a **derivative of the cburnett set** above,
generated by `apps/web/scripts/build-sculpted-pieces.mjs`: the geometry is
unchanged; flat fills are replaced with diagonal ivory/graphite gradients,
per-piece specular highlights and base shading are added, and outlines/detail
lines are recoloured to warm ink/bone tones.

- License: **CC-BY-SA 3.0** (same as the original, as required)
- Original author: Colin M.L. Burnett
- Modifications: PureChess (gradient fills, speculars, recoloured strokes)
